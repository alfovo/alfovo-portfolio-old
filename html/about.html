Nice to meet you, I'm Alex.

For over a year now, I've been the owner and artist behind Camelot Collective, a boutique illustration and web design company based in Atlanta. I am drawn to portraiture, historical counter-narratives, nonlinear differential equations and mass transit systems. My work has been featured at Hodgepodge, the Garden Enthusiast, the 2017 Clarkston Culturefest, the 2017 Grant Park Summer Shade Festival, the 2017 Candler Park Fall Festival and the PeachDish Artist Spotlight Postcard Series.

I spent over three years working as a software engineer before combining it wth my lifelong passion for art and design. I’m also a certified yoga teacher, can knit a mean scarf, make solidly average latte art and was recently cast as a high school extra in Stranger Things.

I’m looking for a full time design job and freelance gigs. Check out my resume and email me if you’re interested in hiring me.
