Allison Turns Sixty

A Birthday Party Invitation

  |     August, 2017   |     Illustration   |     Typography   |     Print  

For her 60th birthday, Allison wanted invitations that were fun, beautiful, and idiosyncratic. I designed double-sided A7 invitations to be printed on matte cardstock, featuring line icons of her favorite things in bright, tropical colors. I also made the icons into magnets to be used as party favors.
