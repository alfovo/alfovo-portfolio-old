Dolla Dolla Biel

Quality Vintage Goods

  |     June, 2017   |     Illustration   |     Branding  

Brian Biel sells about ten pieces of antique furniture, toys and cookware each month through his highly rated eBay business, Dolla Dolla Biel. His goods, which include a collection of antique tube radios, migratory bird hunting stamps and a framed photo of a racoon, are largely bought at auction and sold online for a substantial profit. Given his success, Brian wanted a logo with a vintage, yet simple design that he could easily print on business cards or use on a personal website.
