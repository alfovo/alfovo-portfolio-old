Women of Note

American Women Activists Stationary Set

  |     February, 2017   |     Illustration   |     Women's History     Print  

Shortly after the election, I made portraits of five prominent American women activists from the 20th century — Shirley Chisholm, Sylvia Rivera, Ella Baker, Dolores Huerta, and Billie Jean King — which I sold on greeting cards and prints. I wanted to highlight women activists who, despite their significant contributions to social justice, are relatively unknown. Each greeting card or print featuring their portraits has a short bio on the back. These portraits are for sale as postcards and greeting cards and were featured at Hodgepodge Coffeehouse and Gallery, the 2017 Clarkston Culturefest and the 2017 Grant Park Summer Shade Festival.
