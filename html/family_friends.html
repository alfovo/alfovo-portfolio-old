Family Friends

Doggie Greeting Cards

  December, 2016   |     Illustration   |     Print  

As part of a series for a dog calendar, I created five cheerful pen and ink portraits of the momentary passions of my favorite neighborhood dogs. These portraits are for sale as postcards and greeting cards and were featured at Hodgepodge Coffeehouse and Gallery, the 2017 Clarkston Culturefest and the 2017 Grant Park Summer Shade Festival.
