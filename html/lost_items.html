Where Are My Baby Teeth?

  |     January, 2017   |     Illustration   |     Print  

Iwas inspired by a friend wondering what happened to his baby teeth to make pen and ink sketches of items I have lost under a variety of circumstances. and I imagined a kind of heaven where all lost things are located. I thought of the opening stanza of Elizabeth Bishop’s Poem, "One Art”:

The art of losing isn’t hard to master;
so many things seem filled with the intent
to be lost that their loss is no disaster.
