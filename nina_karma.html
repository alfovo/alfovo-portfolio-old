Nina Karma

Life Coaching Services

  |     July, 2017   |     Illustration   |     Branding  

Nina K. Armah is a perceptive listener with a gift for giving clear, helpful instructions when solicited for advice. After her friends joked that she should spend her time and energy on paying customers, she began advertising her services as a life coach. For her logo, she wanted a monogram of her business name, Nina Karma, that was bold, inspiring and unusual.

With themes of electricity, change and power in mind, I designed Nina’s monogram to suggest a lightning bolt or nuclear radiation warning sign and chose a golden yellow color scheme to evoke happiness, energy, empowerment and wealth. In addition to the logo, I created icon set loosely inspired by Maslow’s hierarchy of needs —a lightning bolt, sock, mug, paint brush and mushroom.
